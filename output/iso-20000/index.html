ISO 20000

ISO 20000 is an international standard for IT service management (ITSM).

Description

ISO 20000 is the first international standard for IT service management (ITSM). It outlines a framework for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an organization’s IT service management system (SMS). The standard helps organizations manage and deliver quality IT services to meet business needs while ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. It emphasizes the importance of aligning IT services with the needs of the business and the necessity of a systematic approach to service management. The framework includes requirements for service delivery, relationship management, resolution processes, and control of the interface between the organization and its service providers. By adopting ISO 20000, organizations can enhance their service quality, improve customer satisfaction, and create a culture of continual improvement, ultimately leading to better performance and competitive advantage in the marketplace.

Examples

  • An IT service provider achieving ISO 20000 certification to demonstrate its commitment to service quality.
  • A company using ISO 20000 principles to streamline its IT service management processes and improve efficiency.

Additional Information

  • ISO 20000 is compatible with other management system standards like ISO 9001 and ISO 27001.
  • Certification to ISO 20000 can improve an organization's credibility and reputation in the market.
