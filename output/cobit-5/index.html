COBIT 5

A comprehensive framework for the governance and management of enterprise IT.

Description

COBIT 5 is a framework developed by ISACA for the governance and management of enterprise IT. It provides a set of best practices, tools, and resources to help organizations achieve their objectives through effective management of information and technology. COBIT 5 encompasses a holistic approach to governance, focusing on the alignment of IT with business goals, risk management, and the delivery of value from IT investments. The framework is designed to be adaptable and scalable, making it suitable for organizations of all sizes and across various industries. COBIT 5 is built on five key principles: meeting stakeholder needs, covering the enterprise end-to-end, applying a single integrated framework, enabling a holistic approach, and separating governance from management. These principles guide organizations in establishing effective governance structures and processes, ensuring that IT supports business objectives while managing risks and resources efficiently.

Examples

  • Implementation of COBIT 5 for IT governance in a financial institution to enhance compliance and risk management.
  • Using COBIT 5 to improve IT service management practices in a healthcare organization.

Additional Information

  • COBIT 5 is widely recognized and adopted by organizations globally.
  • It provides a common language for IT governance and management, facilitating communication between IT and business stakeholders.
